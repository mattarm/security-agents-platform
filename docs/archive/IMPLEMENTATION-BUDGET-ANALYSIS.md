## ⚠️ Budget Risk Factors & Mitigation

### **Cost Risk Analysis**

| Risk Factor | Probability | Impact | Mitigation Strategy |
|-------------|-------------|---------|-------------------|
| **Scope Creep** | High | +$2-4M | Clear requirements, change control process |
| **Integration Complexity** | Medium | +$1-2M | Proof-of-concept for complex integrations |
| **Talent Availability** | Medium | +$1-3M | Early hiring, competitive compensation |
| **Technology Changes** | Low | +$0.5-1M | Flexible architecture, technology evaluation |

### **Budget Optimization Opportunities**

**Potential Savings**:
- **Offshore Development**: $2-3M savings (35% of engineering costs)
- **Phased Rollout**: $1-2M year 1 deferral (reduce initial scope)
- **Open Source Alternatives**: $500K-1M savings (reduce licensing costs)
- **Internal Resources**: $1-2M savings (use existing staff vs external consultants)

**Risk vs Savings Trade-offs**:
- **Offshore**: 20% longer timeline, communication challenges
- **Phased Rollout**: Delayed benefits, potential integration complexity
- **Open Source**: Less enterprise support, potential performance issues
- **Internal Resources**: Less specialized expertise, longer learning curve
